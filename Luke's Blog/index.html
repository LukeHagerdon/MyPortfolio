About

I'm a designer and developer living in south east Texas. I began learning how to code when I was 12 years old and now at age 16 I am launching my own web design business. I am both a front-end and back-end web developer along as a photoshop designer. This is a big step into what I want to become as I get older. Web development has been there as one of my creative outlits and I love seeing all of my projects come to life with a simple amount of code.

My goal for this company is not only to provide websites for people but also offer both design and develoment courses to help you create the website that you have always imagined. I hope to continue on this wonderful treak with web design.
